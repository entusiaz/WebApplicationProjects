ABOUT ME

I’m Ronald Opeyemi Ayodeji Ajiboye. from Ora-Ekiti, Ido Osi Local Government Area of Ekiti State. I graduated with a grade of 4.5/5.0 from d national Aerospace University, Ukraine and I have a Masters degree from the department of Industrial and Production Engineering, with specialization in Engineering Management.

I’ve held top management positions in various multinationals across various sectors with local and international span. I was an Assistant General Manager at ATA LLC, Kiev before I became the Director of Administrative Services of Dogans Sugar Ltd, Lagos.
I’m an Aerospace Research Fellow at Kwara State University. I’m also CEO and co-founder of McRonald AutoDrone Center Ltd.

Furthermore, I’ve worked on projects and social activities including, acting as the Programme Director of the Next Titan Reality TV show, season 5. I co-moderated the Kwara Gubernatorial Forum 2018. In 2013, I was the Chairman, Miss Africa Ukraine. I’m also a YALI Regional Center Alumni.

In addition, I’m a member of the Council of Regulations for Engineering in Nigeria (COREN) and the Royal Aerospace society (RaeS). I have a Diploma in Digital marketing, Strategic Customer relationship Management, Project Management (PMP) and a MBA.

Being an international speaker, I’ve spoken across the Middle East, Europe and Africa. I won the Most Influential Black Man award in Ukraine by Elite-2013
I love singing, cooking, driving, and speaking. I’m interested in mentoring, networking, and logistics. I strongly believe that were firstly humans before religion so every human deserves to be loved.
