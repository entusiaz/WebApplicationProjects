CITY OF REFUGE

CITY OF REFUGE is the site of the orphanage ministry of Child Gospel Mission. The City seeks to provide family, comfort and love for children who have no parents anymore. Apart from making nutrition available, there are existing arrangements for the education and spiritual growth of the children. It is a full-fledged registered orphanage.
